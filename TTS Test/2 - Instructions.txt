Instructions for the test version

First:  capabilities in this version:

a.  Supports custom text to speech on both speakers and soundbars using four commands that are exposed (i.e. accessed in WebCore).

	1)	playTextAsVoiceAndResume(text, volume, voice)
	2)	playTextAsVoicdAndRestore(text, volume, voice)
	The above two support voices from Amazon Polly list of voices.
	3)	playTextAndRestore(text, volume)
	4)	playTextAndResume(text, volume)
b.	Fixed Soundbar play text function.
c.	Added user entered language for SOUNDBAR ONLY.
d.	Added user entered voice for SPEAKER ONLY.
